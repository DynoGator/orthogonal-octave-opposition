# Experiment RES-01: U-Tube Phase Asymmetry Validation

**Objective:** Isolate phase asymmetry ($\delta$) as the causal variable for mass transport.

## Protocol
1. **Control:** Engage emitters at Hard Lock ($90.0^\circ$). Success Criteria: No movement ($\Delta h = 0$).
2. **Shear:** Adjust Phase Offset to Shear Mode ($91.2^\circ$). Success Criteria: Visible rise in Right Arm.
3. **Verify:** Measure TDS of transported fluid. Success Criteria: Lower conductivity than bulk.

## Failure Modes
* **Slippage:** $\delta > 4.0^\circ$ causes coupling collapse (Debye limit).
